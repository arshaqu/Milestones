Services

Our Services

Comprehensive therapies and programs designed to enhance children's development and well-being.

Occupational Therapy

Our Occupational Therapy program helps children develop independence in daily living skills, improve hand-eye coordination, and strengthen motor abilities. Through structured sensory integration activities, children learn to manage their environment effectively and gain confidence in their abilities.

Speech & Language Therapy

Our Speech and Language Therapy enhances children’s communication skills by improving clarity, vocabulary, and comprehension. Therapy sessions also focus on social interaction, expressive language, and confidence-building to help children engage effectively in school and social environments.

Early Intervention

Early Intervention focuses on identifying developmental challenges in children at the earliest stage. Our team provides specialized therapies to address delays in motor, speech, and cognitive development, ensuring children achieve milestones in a timely and supportive manner.

Psychology & Behaviour Therapy

This therapy supports children in managing their emotions, behaviours, and social interactions. Our psychologists use evidence-based practices to help children overcome anxiety, build resilience, and develop healthy coping mechanisms.

Group Therapy

Group Therapy encourages children to learn from peers in a structured environment. It develops teamwork, empathy, and communication skills through engaging activities, helping children improve their social confidence and build meaningful relationships.

Remedial Training

Remedial Training provides customized academic support to children with learning difficulties. Our programs are designed to strengthen reading, writing, and math skills, while also enhancing focus and confidence in a nurturing environment.

Play Skills Development

Structured play sessions help children develop creativity, imagination, and social interactions skills. Through guided activities, they learn problem-solving, teamwork, and confidence-building in a fun and supportive environment.

Brain Gym

Brain Gym exercises enhance focus, memory, and problem-solving abilities. These activities stimulate both sides of the brain, improving concentration, learning speed, and overall cognitive development.

Counselling

Counselling services provide emotional and psychological support for both children and families. Our counsellors address issues like stress, behavioural challenges, and family adjustments with care and empathy.

Home Training

Our experts guide parents in continuing therapy at home, ensuring children receive consistent support. Home training plans include activities that reinforce skills learned during therapy sessions.
